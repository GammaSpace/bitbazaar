SPF 90 FPS

Spend a blazing-hot Toronto summer exploring the wondrous, playful and comedic possibilities of virtual reality.

DMG’s intensive program gives a small group of genderqueer, nonbinary, two spirit, femmes, and women space and one-on-one support to focus on creating new video games, interactive and immersive works.

Over the summer, you will go from idea to interactive VR vignette, with:

  • 40 hours of classroom instruction and hands-on experiences
  • one-on-one mentor support in person and online
  • access to the latest VR hardware and software
  • unlimited access to workstations at Gamma Space
  • presentation, critique and playtesting opportunities
  • opportunities for technical, conceptual and professional development
  • a network of peers committed to supporting your creative success

The program will culminate in a public exhibition at our new gallery space.

Who it’s for

SPF 90 FPS is open only to Toronto-based genderqueer, nonbinary, two spirit, femme folks and/or women over the age of 18. We especially encourage Black, Indigenous and people of colour to apply and prioritize their applications.

This program is designed for creators:

  • of all levels of experience
  • who wish to explore the expressive potential of interactive VR experiences
  • from any background: animators, filmmakers, game designers, video artists, sound artists, performing artists, photographers, fashion designers, cartoonists, etc.!

But I don’t know how to code!

While comfort with computers and an understanding of basic programming concepts will be an asset, no coding, game, VR or art experience is required.

Just be ready to roll up your sleeves! We’ll be diving into intermediate concepts, but you’ll have plenty of support from our experienced instructors and fleet of friendly mentors.
