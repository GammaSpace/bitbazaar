About

Bit Bazaar is a free one-day fair showcasing Toronto's indie game designers, comic artists, zine-makers and DIY creators – with tasty snacks & sips by members of our community.

Meet the makers, hand them money, swap a zine – or just make some cool new friends!

Bit Bazaar is a project by Gamma Space.

Toronto Tech Zines (TTZ) is a unique gathering space for everyone from artists to coders and illustrators to activists— anyone who wants to think about technology on paper.

TTZ is a place for zines about internet infrastructure, love, surveillance, gentrification, dance, futurism, accessibility, games, race, food, geometry, indigineity, instagram, earth, and more.

TTZ is a project by Ones and Zeros.
