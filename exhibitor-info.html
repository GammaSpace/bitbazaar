About

Thank you for being part of this special edition of Bit Bazaar, happening at the Canadian National Exhibition August 25-27. We're thrilled to help inaugurate the Gaming Garage, bringing indie games and comics to Enercare Centre alongside triple A studios, esports, pinball and more. We expect foot traffic in the tens of thousands daily. üò±

Bit Bazaar is just one small part of a very large and complex national event. It will take everyone's cooperation to pull this off with us. We appreciate your attention to the details below! Get in touch if you have any questions.

Insurance

Bit Bazaar is required to have event liability insurance coverage. We have purchased this upfront and split the cost across all Bit Bazaar exhibitors, and each exhibitor will need to chip in $18. You'll receive an invoice with options to pay online via credit card or Interac e-Transfer.

Badges

Badges can be picked up at Gamma Space (862 Richmond Street West, Suite 100):

  • Tuesday, August 22 to Thursday, August 24 between 10 a.m. and 6 p.m.
  • Friday, August 25 between 8 a.m. and 6 p.m.
  • Saturday, August 26 and Sunday, August 27 between 8 a.m. and 2 p.m.

Near the west office marked Tread, there's a box under a Bit Bazaar sign. Take the envelope marked with your group name and check yourself off the list.

You will receive 2 badges per day you are exhibiting. They are single use and you'll be stamped for re-entry the same day. If you have additional staff for the same shift, you‚Äôll need to purchase a general admission badge ($15 until Aug. 17; $19 thereafter) for them.

Badges don‚Äôt have names on them, so you can share them with different people across different days.

Restricted Goods

‚úÖ¬†¬†Stickers are okay! The CNE has made a special exception to allow Bit Bazaar exhibitors to have stickers at their table.

However, they must be offered for sale ‚Äì¬†you may not give them away for free. We recommend they also be packaged (clear plastic bags work great). We will be fined if stickers from any exhibitor are found stuck on the grounds.

üö´¬†¬†Toys, children‚Äôs clothing, cosmetics, and food items are not allowed for sale at your booth. And no raffles or contests.

Tables

  • There's plenty of room behind your table for standing banners. There is curtain wall you can use S-hooks on, as well!
  • Tables will be skirted, but you may bring your own table cloth.
  • Power will be provided, but we recommend bringing your own power bar just in case!
  • If you're sharing a table with another exhibitor, please do not bring a monitor larger than 27".
  • Two chairs per half table, and four per full table will be provided.

Content

If your game, art or comic contains content not suitable for all ages, please be conscientious of the public nature of this event and provide a content warning, and ensure any adult content is not easily visible to passersby.
